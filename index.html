For Cambridge Primary and Lower Secondary Checkpoint
  • 01 Detailed explanation for the checkpoint system including the introduction of the new curriculum framework and examination system.

  • 03 Vocabulary boosters: powerful words and phrases, idioms, etc.
  • 04 Basic text structures for both fiction and non-fiction texts.

  • Detailed explanation for the IGCSE system including the introduction of the new curriculum framework and examination system.
  • Basic and essential grammar and punctuation lessons: word classes, sentence types, tenses, etc.

  • Fundamental writing skills and strategies: text structure, topic sentence, linking phrases, formal and informal style, etc.
